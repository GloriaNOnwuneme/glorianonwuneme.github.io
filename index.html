Why TranslatorTools?

After almost a decade as a freelance translator, I decided to reflect on the lows ( = low-paid machine translation proofreading) and highs ( = getting published by Amazon Crossing). This led me to create a go-to resource for newbie translators.

Click this button to check out TranslatorTools, and get in touch if you need a hand sharing your own unique gifts and experiences with the world.

Why Click-to-Select?

If you spend as much time online as I do, you will at some point have become frustrated with how awkward text selection can be (especially on touch devices).

To fix this, I decided to create a browser extension that lets you click, rather than drag and hold, to select online content.

Click this button to try the demo on any device, and get in touch if you're looking for a developer who is serious about optimising online user experiences!
