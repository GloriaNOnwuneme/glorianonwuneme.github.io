Web Design for Creatives: Ruth Martin

Launched: August 2024

I had the joy of redesigning german-to-english-translation.org for award-winning translator Ruth Martin. My focus was on reorganising her extensive service offerings and client testimonials, ensuring potential clients could quickly recognise and appreciate her expertise and value.

We worked together in the months leading up to the publication of Ruth's translation of Freedom, the memoirs of former German Chancellor Angela Merkel. As she was attending to final revision deadlines, I made sure to implement clear feedback tools and systems. This meant (1) using Google Meet for discussions, (2) keeping communications as focussed and efficient as possible, and (3) sharing interim website demos for review. I also provided a thorough training session on how to independently and easily update her brand-new Squarespace website at the end of our collaboration.

In prioritising partnership, I stayed flexible and sensitive to my client's schedule, adjusting phase delivery timelines as needed, while ensuring that key project goals were still met.

Tools used:

Squarespace [content management + website build]

Google Workspace [project management]

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Browser Plugin Development: Click-to-Select

Launched: September 2024

As a largely self-taught front-end developer, I've built my skills through online courses and hands-on projects. While I deeply enjoy the visual and interactive side of front-end work, I consciously took on the challenge of browser extension development to think beyond aesthetics and focus on core functionality.

I developed the Click-to-Select browser extension from scratch, offering an intuitive alternative to traditional drag-and-drop text selection. This project stretched my abilities, challenging me to focus deeply on user interaction. Click-to-Select, now downloadable from Chrome Web Store, is the result of my knack for identifying user pain points related to information access, then producing elegant, functional solutions that simplify digital tasks. Ultimately, it's about enhancing the user's ability to quickly "take in" and utilise content.

WordPress Blog Creation: Menus of Off Menu

Launched: June 2025

I developed Menus of Off Menu, a dynamic WordPress blog dedicated to compiling guest menus from the popular comedy/food podcast Off Menu. Each individual 'menu' blog post features external recipe links, enabling readers to recreate dishes at home. I also enrich the posts with resources on cookery skills, fun facts, and curated product recommendations. A key achievement was efficiently modifying WordPress template files to speed up the generation of hundreds of reusable resource links and several menu posts without compromising quality and consistency.

By taking time to truly understand the podcast's unique voice and style for this project—its signature blend of whimsy, warmth, and comedic flair— I embraced a colourful, bold design to perfectly match the show's personality. In departing from my usual minimalist style, I made sure to create an online space that would deeply resonate with Menus of Off Menu's well-established fanbase, showcasing my versatility and readiness to adapt my style.

Tools used:

WordPress [website build]

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Generative AI tools [graphic design +

Work Process

If you're looking to create or redesign a website, here's what you can expect from our collaborative journey:

1

Before We Begin

Once you have sent me a request email outlining your vision and initial design preferences, we'll arrange for a focussed 30-minute consultation to define your goals, target audience, and current content challenges.

2

During the Transformation

I establish the foundational structure and information architecture of your new website, focussing on logical content flow. I also refine the website's layout, performance, and user-friendliness, ensuring your message is delivered efficiently. You will receive transparent, weekly status updates, at a minimum, keeping you informed every step of the way.

3

After Launch: Sustained Success

For smaller websites (e.g. websites with up to 4-5 pages), I commit to delivering polished, ready-to-use solutions within a week. Upon completion, I will provide a comprehensive training session on how to effortlessly update and manage your content, along with 30 days of complimentary email support.

About Gloria

I'm Gloria Nneoma Onwuneme, a web developer/designer with a unique background as a former doctor and published translator. My journey into web design began with the thrill of creating my first website, a resource library for new freelance translators. This ignited a passion for crafting engaging and impactful online experiences that truly resonate with users.

My Professional Journey

My experience as a doctor in public UK hospitals and private industry taught me the critical importance of clear, concise communication and the skill of quickly processing and organising vast amounts of information. Translating a biography (Amazon Crossing, 2022) honed my ability to repackage complex narratives for new audiences, ensuring fidelity while optimising for comprehension and flow. Since creating and launching my first website in 2023, I've immersed myself in responsive web design, data analysis, and the use of generative AI, continually expanding my toolkit to build robust, user-centric digital experiences that prioritise information accessibility.
