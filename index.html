If you're looking to create or redesign a website, here's what you can expect:


Before

You fill in a web design request form, available here.

We meet for 30 minutes, during which we'll discuss your goals for your website.

During

Week 1: I create the basic structure of your new website.

Week 2: I refine the website's layout, and work on improving loading speeds and user-friendliness.

You will receive weekly status updates, at a minimum.

After

For individual clients, I deliver websites within 2-3 weeks.

At the end of our collaboration, I'll give you a training session on how to update content with ease, and you will receive 30 days of free email support.

About Me

I'm a Danish-born Nigerian, former doctor and translator, and web developer/designer.

Since launching my first-ever website on Squarespace and coding a back-to-top button from scratch, I've chased the thrill and joy of creating engaging and impactful online experiences.

I've worked as a doctor in public UK hospitals and private industry, delivering frontline care and engaging in clinical research.

I had the privilege of translating a biography about Ester Blenda Nordström from Swedish to English, which was published by Amazon Crossing in 2022.

Ok, back to web-dev real quick: In 2023, I launched my first website, an online resource library for new and experienced translators. Since then, I've completed courses in responsive web design and data analysis.

I'm a keen baker, and have taken to making at least one batch of cookies (or a cake) per week...

...which is why I've deemed it necessary to take up a consistent Pilates practice. For an accurate primer on Pilates, please watch this 3-minute informational video.

I enjoy non-fiction books written by compelling stylists, with topics ranging from authors' lives to anything Brené Brown has to say.
